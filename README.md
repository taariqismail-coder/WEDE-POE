# Riqos Productions

**Official Website** — Underground Music Production Label

---

## About

**Riqos Productions** is a South African creative music ecosystem focused on dark melodic trap, atmospheric production, professional mixing & mastering, cinematic music videos, and artist development.

This website showcases our services, artist roster, visual gallery, and makes it easy for artists to book sessions.

---

## Features

- Fully responsive modern design
- Interactive artist roster with expandable profiles
- Clean services showcase with pricing
- High-quality image & video gallery
- Fast enquiry system (WhatsApp + Email)
- Active navigation highlighting
- Smooth animations and hover effects

---

## Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Vanilla)
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- Static website (no backend)

---

## Pages

- **Home** — Landing & Hero
- **About** — Label story, timeline & roster
- **Services** — Offerings with pricing
- **Gallery** — Visual archives & video highlights
- **Contact** — General enquiries
- **Enquiry** — Booking form & price calculator
